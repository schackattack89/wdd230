Overview

Purpose

The Scott County Chamber of Commerce strives to build a community of resources for its members. Our caring members share their talents, resources and expertise because they are not only members, they serve as LEADERS in the community by supporting one another through their means available. It doesn't matter if you are a large corporation, a franchise, a small retail store, an at-home business or a member of the community that wants to get involved. The Scott County Chamber of Commerce is here for you. Join our members and join our community and be part of the success.

Audience

All those interested in the furtherance of community businesses - both small and large scale - can find help and support from the Scott County Chamber of Commerce.
